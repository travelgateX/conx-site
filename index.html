WELCOME TO CON-X 2023

Uncut: Now it’s personal

"Uncut: Now it's personal", is the name of the conference that opens the doors to inspiring personal experiences in the travel and tourism business sector, as well as inspiring entrepreneurs who have succeeded against all odds in other areas. It will also be a moment to reflect in a round table on personal treatment in the face of depersonalization with robots, artificial intelligence, new trends.
Are you ready to learn, share and do business? Welcome to CON-X 2023!

The world's most exclusive travel distribution events

Con-X is a handcrafted and by invitation only all day conference and networking event limited to 250 C-Level decision makers from some of the most relevant and game changer companies in the travel distribution sector. After-X is a top level networking and party event with 650+ participants from the distribution sector with the focus on dynamizing contacts among participants. They both take place in Mallorca every year in two consecutive days and in unique and specially selected scenarios.
