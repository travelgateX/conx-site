Adapt. Pivot. Grow.

We all hold good memories of that beautiful day in Mallorca when Con-X was born. It seems like a lifetime ago, so much has happende since. Mallorca on the 7th of May 2019 was a very special time and place to be.

You demanded it. We heard you. Now Con-X is back!

Our first edition featured 18 speakers, 13 sponsors and 350 attendees from 45 countries, represeting 223 different companies.

The date is set for our second edition, 31 May 2022.
