WELCOME TO CON-X 2022

ADAPT. PIVOT. GROW.

We all hold good memories of that beautiful day in Mallorca when Con-X was born. It seems like a lifetime ago, so much has happened since. Mallorca on the 7th of May 2019 was a very special time and place to be.

You demanded it. We heard you. Now Con-X is back.

Our first edition featured 18 speakers, 13 sponsors and 350 attendees from 45 countries, representing 223 companies.

The date is set for our second edition: 31 May 2022

As the industry recovers and prepares for the "rebound of the century", it’s time to celebrate that light at the end of the tunnel.
