We all hold good memories of that beautiful day in Mallorca. Has it been a year already? A year and a half? It seems like a lifetime, or at least a Main Era—the kind of peak that never comes again. Maybe it meant something, maybe not, not in the big swing of things, then again, it might have meant something to someone... but no explanation, no mix of words or music or memories can touch that sense of knowing that you were there and alive in that corner of time and the world. Whatever it meant...
